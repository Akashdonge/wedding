Day 1

Haldi Ceremony

April 19, 2025 7:00 PM Shivalaya, Ankali Traditional Yellow Attire

The auspicious Haldi ceremony marks the beginning of our wedding celebrations. During this traditional ritual, a paste made from turmeric (haldi), sandalwood, and rose water is applied to the bride and groom by family members to bless the couple with prosperity and ward off evil. The yellow color symbolizes purity and is a part of the holy preparation for the wedding ceremony.
